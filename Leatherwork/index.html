Bags

Tote Bag w/Indonesian Batik Lining

Black leather tote with a tan leather base and gold hardware. Features a stunning Indonesian batik fabric interior lining in black and gold, an interior zipper pocket, and sturdy riveted straps.

Leather Tote with Coffee Sack Panels

A tan leather tote built around panels cut from authentic raw-coffee shipping sacks. The jute body of the bag is left fully exposed, complete with the original screen-printed origin art: bold graphics, cooperative markings, and export text from producers around the world. Gold rivets and a turn-lock clasp finish the hardware.

Leather Tote with Scottish Tartan Panels

A tan leather tote featuring panels of authentic Scottish tartan cloth imported directly from Scotland. The deep green, navy, red, and white plaid is framed by tan leather trim, riveted straps, and a gold turn-lock clasp, blending Old World textile heritage with handcrafted leatherwork.

Roll Top Backpack

A full leather roll top backpack built for everyday carry. The roll top closure cinches down to seal the main compartment and expands when you need extra capacity. Sturdy leather straps, riveted hardware, and tight hand stitching throughout make this a bag built to last a lifetime.
