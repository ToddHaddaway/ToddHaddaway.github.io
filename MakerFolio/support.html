MakerFolio

Portfolio App for Makers

📖 About MakerFolio

MakerFolio is a portfolio app built for makers, crafters, and builders. Capture and organize your projects with photos, descriptions, materials, time invested, and links — all in one place. Whether you make furniture, leather goods, electronics, or anything in between, MakerFolio helps you document your work and showcase what you've built.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data backed up?

Your projects are stored on your device. Your data is backed up as part of your normal iPhone backup — whether that’s an iCloud backup or a backup to your computer via Finder. Note that MakerFolio does not currently sync across multiple Apple devices.

Can I share my projects?

Yes. You can export any project as a .makerfolio file and share it via AirDrop, Messages, Mail, or any other sharing method. Recipients who have MakerFolio installed can import it directly.

Will MakerFolio work on my iPad?

Yes, MakerFolio is a universal app and runs on both iPhone and iPad.
