Privacy Policy — Effective February 24, 2026

Your privacy matters. MakerFolio is designed to keep your data on your device and under your control. This policy explains exactly what information we collect — and what we do not.

🚫 No Personal Data Collected

MakerFolio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All project data, photos, and content you create in the app remain on your device and in your personal iCloud account (if you have iCloud sync enabled). We never see any of it.

👁️ No User Tracking

We do not track you across apps or websites. MakerFolio does not use any tracking technologies, fingerprinting, or behavioral profiling of any kind.

📊 No Analytics

MakerFolio does not integrate any analytics frameworks or services. We do not collect crash reports, usage statistics, session data, or any other form of telemetry.

📢 No Third-Party Advertising

MakerFolio contains no advertisements and does not share any data with advertising networks or third-party marketing services.

🛒 In-App Purchases

Any purchases made within MakerFolio are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not handle payment information directly. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles purchase data.
