MakerFolio

For makers of all kinds — woodworkers, sewers, knitters, builders, leatherworkers, and beyond. Document your creations, track your time and materials, and build a portfolio of everything you've made. Your projects deserve a better home than your camera roll!

What it does

📁

Organize Your Projects

Keep every project in one place with photos, descriptions, and categories.

📸

Photo Documentation

Attach multiple photos to capture your build process from start to finish.

📝

Build Notes

Write detailed notes about your process so you can recreate or improve your work later.

🔩

Materials & Tools

Record the materials, supplies, and tools used so nothing gets forgotten.

📐

Measurements

Store dimensions, quantities, and specs alongside your project for future reference.

🔗

Save Links

Attach reference links, tutorials, and resources directly to your projects.

📤

Share Your Work

Export and share any project as a file via AirDrop, Messages, or Mail.
