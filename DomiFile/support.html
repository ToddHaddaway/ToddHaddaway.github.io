DomiFile

Home Management App

🏠 About DomiFile

DomiFile is a home management app built for homeowners who want to stay on top of everything — without the chaos of scattered notes, lost receipts, and forgotten warranties. Document every room, track appliances and warranties, set maintenance reminders, log contractors, manage a home inventory, and more. All your home's information, in one place, on your device.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much does DomiFile cost?

DomiFile is a one-time purchase — no subscriptions, no in-app purchases. Pay once and you get full, unlimited access to every feature, forever.

What iPhone or iPad do I need?

DomiFile requires iOS 17 or later. It's a universal app and runs on both iPhone and iPad. On iPhone it uses a clean tab-based layout; on iPad it becomes a full sidebar navigation experience.

Is my data backed up?

Your data is stored locally on your device and backed up as part of your normal iPhone backup — whether that’s an iCloud backup or a backup via Finder on your Mac. Restoring from backup will bring all your DomiFile data with it. DomiFile does not currently sync across multiple Apple devices simultaneously.

Can I manage more than one property?

Yes. DomiFile supports multiple properties — a primary residence, a rental, a vacation home — all from one app. Switch between properties instantly.

Does DomiFile send my data anywhere?

No. Everything in DomiFile is stored locally on your device. No account is required, no subscription, and no data is sent to the cloud or any third party.

Can I use DomiFile on my iPad?

Yes. DomiFile is a universal app and runs natively on both iPhone and iPad, with a full sidebar navigation layout optimized for the larger screen.
