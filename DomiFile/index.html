DomiFile

Know your home, inside and out.

The home management app for homeowners who want to stay on top of everything — without the chaos of scattered notes, lost receipts, and forgotten warranties.

One-time purchase  ·  No subscriptions  ·  Your data stays on your device

Coming to the App Store

What it does

🏠

Document Every Room

Record paint colors, finishes, and inventory for every room. Touch up a wall or replace a fixture without guessing.

🔔

Never Miss Maintenance

Set recurring reminders for furnace filters, gutter cleaning, HVAC service, and more. DomiFile notifies you when tasks are due.

🔧

Track Every Appliance

Store model numbers, serial numbers, purchase dates, and warranty expirations. Ready the moment something breaks.

👷

Your Contractor List

Save contact info, trade, star ratings, and job history for every plumber, electrician, and service provider you use.

📋

Home Inventory

Photograph and document your valuables — electronics, furniture, jewelry — for insurance purposes. Know exactly what you own.

🛒

Supplies & Parts

Track filter sizes, bulb specs, and battery types so you always know exactly what to buy before heading to the hardware store.

🌿

Yard Log

Record every fertilizer application, mulch delivery, and weed treatment — including dates, quantities, and products used.

✅

Seasonal Checklists

Build reusable checklist templates for seasonal tasks, move-in inspections, and storm prep. Spin up a new checklist in seconds.

🏘️

Multiple Properties

Manage a primary residence, a rental, and a vacation home all from one app. Switch between properties instantly.

📱

iPhone & iPad Native

A clean tab-based layout on iPhone. A full sidebar navigation experience on iPad. Native, intuitive, and easy to browse.
